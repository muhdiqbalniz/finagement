👋 Welcome to Finagement!

Let's get your monthly dashboard ready in 2 quick steps:

💵 Step 1: Set Up Your Income
Head to the Income tab. Enter your base pay and any allowances or dynamic deductions to find your true Nett Pay, then divide it into your main financial buckets.
📦 Step 2: Establish Expense Budgets
Use the Expenses tab to map out custom individual targets under Needs, Wants, or Savings (like Rent, Groceries, or Investments).
